About Me

This page tells the story of my life, the experiences that shaped who I am, and the values that guide me forward.

Early Years

I grew up with a curiosity that always pushed me to ask “why” and “how.” From an early age, I explored the unknown— books, outdoor adventures, and hands-on experiments with whatever I could find. My family’s encouragement helped turn small discoveries into habits of learning. Those moments instilled the belief that growth comes from curiosity and persistence.

School Years

School focused that curiosity into goals. I gravitated to math and science for their clarity, while literature and philosophy trained me to think critically. Leading group projects and mentoring younger students taught me that leadership is as much about listening as guiding. Not every step was smooth, but setbacks built resilience, patience, and determination.

Passions

Beyond class, competing in volleyball shaped how I define teamwork and discipline. Fitness became a daily practice and a way to help others reach their goals. Debate and Model UN sharpened my ability to analyze issues and advocate for solutions. Each passion added a layer to my identity—independence balanced with collaboration.

Today & Future

Today I’m shaped by curiosity and responsibility. Academics give me the tools to ask bold questions; extracurriculars give me the resilience to pursue answers. Looking ahead, I want to apply what I’ve learned in ways that help communities I’m part of, while continuing to challenge myself academically and personally.
